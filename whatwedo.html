OBGYN department

The department of Obstetrics and Gynecology was functioning since 2016. The department started its work by teaching medical and other health science students, undertaking clinical researches but started rendering clinical service to the community in January, 2017. The department currently offers academic courses to postgraduate obstetrics and gynecology specialty program, undergraduate medical students including interns, clinical year 1 and 2 students, health officer and other medical intern from Somaliland and china who are doing their internship in our hospital.

OBGYN department

Clinical care is rendered in different department of the hospital including inpatient care with capacity of more than 62 beds (With dedicated wards for gynecology, abortion and its complications and hyperemesis patients, antepartum and postpartum patients, labor and delivery patients), OPD (1 Regular OPD, 2 OPD Referral clinics for Gynecology and high risk patients), 2 OPD for ANC, and one family planning OPDs). In addition, major and minor operative care is given ij clinical services.

OBGYN department

The department also opened 2014 E.C maternal ICU with 3 bed and 2 functioning mechanical ventilators run by well-trained staffs. The staff of the department includes 9 senior General gynecologists, general Practitioners, 8 residents and one subspecialty fellowship staff who is on study leave. The department has been aggressively engaged in improving the patient care and the academic activities by hiring specialists.

Internal Medicine

There are two major wards for inpatients in the department of Internal Medicine, Medical A ward which has male and female side and Medical B ward which has male and female side. The total in patient capacity is 74 beds. The rooms are well illuminated and ventilated, the inpatient wards are not crowded that enough space is available between adjacent beds. There is also isolation room for some patients to be quarantined/isolated. The department of internal medicine is currently properly functioning and engaged in giving clinical and academic services. The staff of the department includes 9 Internists, 1 cardiologist ,1 Neurologist,1 nephrologist (guest) ,5 General Practitioners, 16 Internal Medicine residents ,44 Nurses and other supportive staffs. The department has launched postgraduate specialty program since 2013 EC.
