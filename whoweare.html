Our History

We heal one person every minute. Since 1994.

EMERGENCY is an independent and neutral association, founded in 1994 to offer free, high-quality medical-surgical care to victims of war, landmines and poverty.

EMERGENCY promotes a culture of peace, solidarity and respect for human rights.

Our commitment is possible thanks to the contribution of thousands of volunteers and supporters who choose to stay with us every day.

HOW WE OPERATE

All EMERGENCY structures are designed, built and managed by specialized international staff, also engaged in the training of local personnel. In order for everyone to have the right to free medical care, EMERGENCY:

It offers completely free assistance

It guarantees care to anyone in need, without political, ideological or religious discrimination

It gives a high quality health response

Train local staff until they achieve complete operational autonomy

8%

The expenses for the structure represent 8% of the total: our commitment to reduce them as much as possible allows us to allocate 86% of what we collect to our hospitals and to the promotion of a culture of peace. We invest the remaining 6% to raise other funds to support our projects.
